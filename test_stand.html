Hydraulic Test Stand

Figure: Schematic of New Hydraulic Test Stand with Sensors

Problem statement:
 A local company has an old test stand which already used for more than thirty years. Based on the development of their business, the conpany needs a brand new test stand to test the mechanical parts.

Objective:
 Design a brand new hydraulic test stand based on customer requirements.

Customer Requirements:
 Req 1. The new test stand has multiple main circuits and an additional circuit.
Req 2. The new test stand can generate up to 690 Bar system pressure.
Req 3. The flow rate of the test stand is based on the power limited.
Req 4. Use electric as the power source.
Req 5. The dimension of new stand is same as the old stand.
Req 6. The new test stand has sensors and controls to shut down the test and the sensors are stand above safety limits.
Req 7. The new test stand has the ability to mount pumps and motors.
Req 8. The new test stand has a shield to prevent high pressure leaking oil from the testing area.
Req 9. The trough of the new test stand is large enough to hold largest pumps and motors.
Req 10. The new test stand has variable flow control.
Req 11. The new test stand has variable system pressure.
Req 12. Use company's products as much as possible.
Req 13. The new test stand has recovery pump to return leaked oil
Req 14. Good serviceability
Final Design:

The final design is shown in the first figure. For this project, we did not built a prototype due to the budget problem.
However, a schematic has been drawn, and we did simulation for our design to verify our concept.
The schematic and simulation of this project generated by using Automation Studio.
