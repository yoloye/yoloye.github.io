Footwear Sole Cleaner


Problem statement:

There is no efficiency cleaning staff at the entrance of the university's engineering building. In winter, snow and road salt carried by people's shoe, and they made building's floor dirty. This project aims to find a method to separate snow, road salt and shoe.


Objective:

Separate materials on footwear soles efficiently.

Final Design:


The final design will be placed on the orange area in the first figure to substitute the floor tile.
When people step on it, the long tile will move down a safety distance.
It transfers people's potential energy to kinetic energy in order to drive the shoe brush.
By using this method, electrical energy will not be needed.
This design is easy to open, and janitors can easily open the design and clean it.

Learnt from Project:

Improve Solidworks Skills.
Improve project management skill.
Design for safety.
Design for regulatory.
Design for sustainability and environment.
