Panasonic Optical Storage Research Institute
Engineer Intern

Panasonic Optical Storage Research Institute is owned by Hualu-Panasonic co., Ltd. It designs high capacity and low-cost data storage devices for industrial companies. The optical storage device constituted by multiple discs array, and a disc array constituted by twelve blue-ray discs. The institute first uses RAID 0, 5 and 6 technology on optical storage media. The reading and writing speed can reach up to 216MB/s.
