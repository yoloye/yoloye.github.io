Product Handling Machine

Problem statement:
 A local company was experiencing issues with product damage (“nicks and dings”) related to parts evacuation from a FineBlanking tool and handling them into a production bin. This problem waste them about 90,000CAD per year.

Objective:
 Reducing or eliminating unacceptable indents on FineBlanking parts.

Final Design:

The final design looks like a box with three slides. The design mainly uses three slides to reduce the kinetic energy and potential energy of the fine blanking parts.
The design has a transparent plastic window which allows the worker to check the design without open it. The holes on slides can reduce the weight of the whole design, also, these holes can allowing small "coin" from fine blanking parts passing through. The angle also can be adjusted based on different fine blanking parts so that this design can be used for different products.
